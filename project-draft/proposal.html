Skincare App

The Problem

The beauty industry is booming and there are an overwhelming amount of products available to consumers. This choice overload makes it difficult for people to find products that are suitable for their needs including but not limited to: price, skin type, and ingredients. In an effort to have some guidance, many people turn to social media such as YouTube (e.g., beauty channels) or Reddit (e.g., skincare subreddits) to ask for advice and search for answers. However, social media often has the problem of information overload as well.

Skin conditions on the face can affect the quality of life for individuals, making them feel self-conscious, unattractive, and focused on their skin; in a previous study, individuals have reported that their skin condition impacted their daily activities and forced them to put in more effort to hiding pigments on their skin. The most common skin conditions are not only difficult to treat, but many abnormal pigmentations, such as melasma, have a high probability for relapse and can appear permanently from changes in hormone balances. The unintended effects from living with physical skin conditions can be detrimental to the mental health of the people that it affects.

While there are websites such as Sephora that suggest products to users through a skincare quiz, they do not address many areas such as ingredient or cruelty-free preferences. Furthermore, tools such as skin-matching are often brand-specific and do not compare across products from other companies. With so many product options and so little adequate tools, searching for the right product can be exhausting. We hope to alleviate the source of stress by filtering out superfluous information about products and displaying just the right amount of information needed to help users find the perfect product.

App Description

Users

The users of the application include any individuals who are dissatisfied with their facial skin and have a desire to treat or cover the physical appearance of certain skin conditions. Users will come from various backgrounds and ages. The website can be accessed by any individual from the general public. Skin conditions can affect any type of individual; therefore, this application will appeal to a diverse range of users who are interested in skin treatment.

Data Information

The information the users will be viewing are skin product suggestions deriving from the annual Allure Best of Beauty Awards list. The solution will aggregate data from Allure’s award-winning skin products across various years and present different alternatives to the user based on best match. Each product will list important factors for filtering and comparing such as ingredients, color ranges, links to purchase, cost, cruelty-free, or vegan.

Interactive Features

Our application will be used by anyone who struggles with getting information about what skincare products to use. With so many products available, finding products that will benefit each person"s skin is very difficult. Not using the right skincare products can end up damaging your skin. This website will be for ordinary people who want to find skincare products that will help them. With this website, users will not have spend tons of money on products that don’t work for them or spend hours researching for what products to buy. This website will also help people who are just starting out with skincare and are not sure what direction they want to go in. This website will narrow the field of skincare and make it more accessible to our users. The website will use a survey to collect data about the skin type of the user. It will then show the different products available that would best suit their skin type. The website will show why it is compatible, the price, and where to buy the product. The data used will be collected from the Allure Beauty awards. Over 300 products were awarded for being the best in beauty. We will create our own data set from these products. On our website, users will interact with a survey to collect information about their skin condition. They will click on which option describes their skin the best. We will have 4 multiple choice questions for them to answer. After the survey, results will be shown. The user will be able to click on the result to be led to a page where they can shop for the product.

Solution

Skin conditions typically cannot be cured, therefore our proposed application will help users identify any skin complications and guide them towards the best products on the market to cover or treat their condition. This application will:

  • Enable the user to take a self-assessment to identify skin conditions for treatment
  • Suggest alternative solutions across various top brands, based on the condition identified by the user
  • Allow the user to compare the suggested products based on different factors such as ingredients, cost, range of options, and more
  • Cater the user"s search using filters find the most effective product for their skin
By providing different resources and alternatives for individuals with physical skin imperfections, the application serves as a support system to increase confidence and improve the quality of life for its users.
